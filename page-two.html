The Origin of NFT's

The first instance of a Non-Fungible Token being mentioned, was in 2012. This was, the one and only Colored Coin.

Scroll

A Colored coin, is where you take a very small amount of bitcoin, which does not have much value. It is marketed, to have special meaning to its monetary value. This is like adding a stamp to the bitcoin, which makes it unique as a token. It could be as small as a single Satoshi.

Scroll

With colored coins, you can purchase property, ability to distribute your own crypto currency, selling shares of a company, and other virtual assets.

Scroll Scroll

Colored Coins have lost their attention, and became obsolete. However, this laid out the groundwork for a healthy future in the NFT space, and the idea to be developed later.
