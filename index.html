Explore The World Of NFTs

What is an NFT?

NFT’s are unique and scarce cryptographic items, that are on the block chain – A digitally native file, for a blockchain network.

The History Of NFT's ↓

  • 2012 Coloured Coins were the first ever time instance that anything like the Non-Fungible Token was mentioned. Created by Meni Rosenfeld (Chairman at Israeli Bitcoin Association), Vitalik Buterin (Co-founder of Ethereum), and Yoni Assia (CEO of E Toro). The Coloured Coins were created during a time that Bitcoin was at $5.27, at the start of 2012. At the time, it was By the end of the year, it was $13.30.
  • 2014 Counterparty was the first large scale actualization of an open-source protocol, on top of the bitcoin block chain.
  • 2015-16 Counterparty system used to create the meme economy. These were one of the first NFT’s actively being traded online.
  • 2017 Ethereum was getting popular, ERC721 was the most effective way to build non-Fungible unique assets.
  • 20 April 2017 Spells of Genesis – The first blockchain mobile game ever made.
  • 23 June 2017 Crypto Punks launched. 10,000-pixel portraits were handed out for free.
  • 28 November 2017 Crypto Kitties was popular in the mainstream. The world’s first block chain game on Ethereum, where players could collect, breed and sell virtual cats.
  • February 2020 Decentraland Mana is released. A virtual platform where you can trade land.
  • March 11, 2021 Beeple’s NFT sold for highest amount ever. ($69.3 million).
